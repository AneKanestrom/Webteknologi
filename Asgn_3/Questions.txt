Questions 
1. What is the difference between position: absolute and position: fixed?

position: absolute depends on nearby elements, while position: fixed stays in one spot on the screen even when you scroll.

2. With CSS3, how do we access the third element within another element?

:nth-child() pseudo-class. We use: .parent-element :nth-child(3)
